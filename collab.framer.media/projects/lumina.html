Lumina introduces a new dimension in AI chat, bringing sophisticated conversational technology directly to you. Leading the way in digital dialogue, Lumina's advanced platform ensures unparalleled responsiveness and clarity.

The future begins here

Collab played a pivotal role in shaping the early stages of the Lumina AI chat app, focusing on the development of its brand identity, product design, and website design. Their expertise was instrumental in crafting a cohesive and compelling user experience, which helped secure $6 million in initial funding.

This strategic collaboration not only provided crucial financial support but also established Lumina as a leader in the realm of advanced AI communication tools, setting it apart as a leader in innovative digital interactions.
